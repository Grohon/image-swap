## Third-Party Services

### Picsum Photos
The extension uses the Picsum Photos API to generate random placeholder images.

- **Service:** Picsum Photos
- **Website:** https://picsum.photos
- **Purpose:** Provides random, high-quality placeholder images
- **License:** Images from Unsplash (Free to use under Unsplash License)
- **Privacy:** See Picsum Photos' privacy policy at https://picsum.photos

**Note:** Image requests are made directly from your browser to Picsum Photos. We do not intermediate or store these requests.
